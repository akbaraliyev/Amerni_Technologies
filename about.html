About us

Your Partner in
Innovation

Amerni is a Canadian based company which is operating in Dubai and Qatar to provide cost effective with higher ROI end to end IT Solutions across the Middle East and Africa Region. The Core product of Amerni Technologies is an ERP named Orbit which is built using proven Oracle Technology.

The strength of the product is the customization which can be done as per the business needs without compromising business standards, quality and security. Amerni Technologies also develops a Bespoke ORBIT solution for Front office, various modules along with its standard back office modules which is specifically designed for MENA region which includes the modules such as Finance & Fleet, Assets, HR & Payroll.

In a nut shell we are superior in analyzing client requirements and transforming into tailor made customized solutions which will help our customers to streamline their business processes through complete business automation solutions.

Our dedicated System Engineers team works constantly in upgrading our system to stay on the cutting edge of latest developments in the Technology, Systems and Practices so that our clients don't have to.

Powering Digital Transformation Through ORBITS 365

At Amerni Technologies, we understand that every enterprise runs on complex operational workflows. From fleet movement and mobility operations to financing, finance, HR, procurement, inventory, workshop management, mobile application use, integrations, automation, compliance, and executive reporting, organizations need systems that are scalable, integrated, compliant, and built for real business execution. ORBITS 365 is designed to address this need.

It is an integrated enterprise platform that brings multiple business functions into a single, structured and scalable environment. The solution helps in automating manual dependencies, improve visibility, strengthen internal controls, and make faster, data-driven decisions.

With the growing regional demand for digital transformation, Amerni Technologies also enables multi-integration with approved AXF platforms, helping enterprises prepare for regulatory requirements through secure invoice exchange, validation, reconciliation, and ERP-connected compliance workflows.

Amerni Technologies enabled ERP, integrated RPA and process automation to streamline repetitive workflows, reduce manual intervention, and improve operational speed, accuracy, and control.
