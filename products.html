Our Products

ORBITS - Oracle Based Information Technology Solutions

Powering Smarter Enterprise Operations Through ERP, Mobility & Automation

ORBITS 365 is a unified enterprise platform designed to connect operational, financial, HR, procurement, inventory, mobility, and reporting functions into one integrated digital ecosystem.

Taxi, Fleet & Mobility Management

Enable end-to-end management of Limousine, Car Rentals, and Bus Operations through integrated workflows covering bookings, contracts, lease calculations, vehicle allocation, driver assignment, customer management, billing coordination, revenue tracking, and operational reporting.

Key Features

  • Booking and reservation management for limousine, car rental, and bus operations
  • Customer and corporate client profile management
  • Contract creation and operational linkage with vehicles, drivers, rates, billing terms, and service conditions
  • Lease Calculator to support lease computation, rental values, contract pricing, insurance, service and maintenance cost allocation, administrative charges, and billing-related calculations
  • Vehicle allocation based on availability, category, customer requirement, and contract type
  • Driver assignment for chauffeur-driven, limousine, and bus operations
  • Rental period tracking with start date, return date, extensions, renewals, and usage monitoring
  • Revenue tracking by booking, contract, customer, vehicle, driver, and business unit
  • Billing coordination with finance for invoicing, receivable follow-up, and contract-linked revenue recognition
  • Operational status tracking from booking confirmation to trip closure, contract completion, or vehicle return
  • Reports for booking pipeline, active contracts, lease calculation summaries, revenue, utilization, and customer performance

Port Management

Mobile Apps, Portals & Integrations

ORBITS extends ERP access through mobile apps, employee self-service portals, customer/vendor portals, and secure third-party integrations. It supports field operations, approvals, driver and employee requests, external system connectivity, and real-time business updates covering utilization, manpower, and key operational KPIs.

RPA & Process Automation

ORBITS uses RPA and process automation to reduce manual effort in high-volume, repetitive business activities such as traffic fines, toll charges, parking transactions, approvals, reconciliations, and data uploads. This improves accuracy, processing speed, audit control, and overall operational efficiency.

E-Invoicing & ASP Integration

ORBITS supports e-Invoicing readiness through structured invoice data, tax field validation, ERP-to-ASP connectivity, and compliance-oriented workflows. The solution helps businesses align with regional e-Invoicing requirements such as UAE FTA and Saudi ZATCA mandates while maintaining invoice tracking and audit visibility.
